Cognitive Multimedia's mission is to provide the public a platform to buy digital products with ease.

Who we are

The creators behind Cognitive Multimedia is a group of students from University Malaysia Pahang. This website was established on the 15th of October in 2021 with a mission - to provide people high-quality digital products through the Internet.


Our goals

We aim to provide users a wide range of high quality digital products so that users can shop comfortably at home with ease. Our goal is to make comparing prices and products a less troublesome task.
